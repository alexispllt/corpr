Corpr - Help & Support (FAQ)

Welcome to the Corpr Help Center. We've compiled a list of the most frequently asked questions to help you get the most out of your personal financial terminal. If you can't find the answer you're looking for, feel free to contact our support team.

1. General & Account

What is Corpr?

Corpr is an institutional-grade financial terminal designed for your iPhone. It helps investors of all levels analyze stocks using 12 dynamic KPI metrics, build smart simulated portfolios, and master investing through interactive educational courses.

Is Corpr free to use?

Yes! You can download Corpr and use many of its core features—including basic stock search, educational courses, and portfolio simulation—for free. We also offer a Premium subscription for users who want unlimited access to deep AI-driven analysis, advanced metrics, and unlimited portfolio tracking.

How do I reset my learning progress?

If you want to start fresh, you can reset all your progress, quiz scores, and simulated portfolios.

  1. Open the Settings menu.
  2. Scroll down to the Learning section.
  3. Tap Reset Progress and confirm. (Warning: This action cannot be undone!)

How do I delete my account?

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time.

  1. Go to Settings.
  2. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  3. Confirm the deletion. All your personal data, portfolios, and progress will be instantly wiped from our servers.

2. Subscriptions & Premium

What is included in Corpr Premium?

Corpr Premium unlocks the full power of our financial terminal. It includes:

  • Unlimited "Analyst's Take" summaries: AI-powered executive overviews of any public company.
  • Advanced KPI Metrics: Deep dive into Valuation, Profitability, Financial Strength, and Growth.
  • Unlimited Portfolios & Watchlists: Track as many stocks and simulated portfolios as you want.
  • Ad-Free Experience: Focus purely on your financial analysis without interruptions.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed directly by Apple through your Apple ID.

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone.
  2. Tap your Name at the top.
  3. Tap Subscriptions.
  4. Select Corpr and tap Cancel Subscription.

Note: You will retain access to Premium features until the end of your current billing cycle.

I switched phones. How do I restore my Premium purchase?

If you have an active subscription but it isn't showing up:

  1. Go to the Profile or Premium tab in the app.
  2. Look for the Restore Purchases button and tap it.
  3. You may be asked to authenticate with your Apple ID.

3. Features & Gameplay

Are the stock prices and data real-time?

Corpr provides highly accurate market data. Prices for most major exchanges are either real-time or delayed by up to 15 minutes, depending on the specific exchange's rules and market hours.

How do simulated portfolios work?

Simulated portfolios (Paper Trading) allow you to practice investing without risking real money. You can "buy" and "sell" stocks at their current market prices and track how your investments would perform over time. It's the perfect way to test strategies before hitting the real market.

What is the "Analyst's Take"?

The "Analyst's Take" is an automated, AI-driven executive summary. It reads through complex financial data and generates a clear, concise overview of a company’s quantitative health, saving you hours of manual research.

What are the 12 KPI metrics?

We break down complex financial statements into 12 easy-to-understand Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) spread across four categories:

  1. Valuation (Is the stock cheap or expensive?)
  2. Profitability (Is the company making money efficiently?)
  3. Financial Strength (Does the company have too much debt?)
  4. Growth (Is the company expanding its revenue and earnings?)

4. Privacy & Security

Is my personal data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your account. Your passwords are encrypted, and we do not store any sensitive banking information, as all portfolios in the app are currently simulated.

Do you sell my data to third parties?

No. We do not sell your personal data or email addresses to third parties for tracking or marketing purposes. For more details, please read our Privacy Policy.

How do I manage my notifications?

You can control which notifications you receive directly from your device settings.

  1. Go to Settings within the Corpr app.
  2. Tap Notifications under the System section. This will open your iPhone's native settings where you can toggle alerts on or off.
