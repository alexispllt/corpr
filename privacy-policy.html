Privacy Policy for Corpr

Effective Date: May 28, 2026

Welcome to Corpr! This Privacy Policy explains how Alexis Pollet ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use the Corpr mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your personal data and your right to privacy in compliance with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect via the App includes:

1.1 Personal Data

Demographic and other personally identifiable information that you voluntarily give to us when registering for the App or when participating in various activities related to the App, such as:

  • Email Address: Used for account creation, authentication, and password recovery.
  • Username and Display Name: Used to identify you within the App's social and community features.
  • Profile Picture (Avatar): Images or icons you upload to personalize your account.
  • Bio: Any optional text you provide to describe yourself on your public profile.

1.2 Financial and Usage Data

  • Portfolios and Collections: Data regarding the simulated financial portfolios, holdings, and watchlists you create within the App. Note: We do not connect to your real bank accounts, nor do we request or store real credit card numbers, brokerage credentials, or real transactional banking data.
  • Learning Progress: Information regarding your completion of in-app educational courses and quiz scores.
  • Search History: Recent stock tickers or companies you have searched for to improve your user experience.
  • Social Interactions: Data regarding other users you follow, your followers, and your public portfolios.

1.3 Device and Diagnostic Data

We may automatically collect device information when you access the App, such as:

  • Device Information: Operating system, device model, and unique device identifiers.
  • Log and Crash Data: Error logs and crash reports to help us identify and fix bugs (processed via Firebase Crashlytics).

2. How We Use Your Information

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you via the App to:

  1. Create and manage your account.
  2. Provide, operate, and maintain the App's core features (simulated portfolios, company analysis, AI summaries).
  3. Process your premium subscription purchases (handled securely via Apple In-App Purchases).
  4. Facilitate social interactions between users (followers, following, public portfolio sharing).
  5. Improve the App, troubleshoot bugs, and analyze usage trends.
  6. Send you push notifications regarding stock price alerts and social interactions (only if you have opted in).
  7. Respond to customer service requests and support needs.

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may share information we have collected about you in certain situations:

  • Third-Party Service Providers: We use third-party services to facilitate our App, such as Google Firebase (for database hosting, authentication, and crash reporting) and financial data providers (like AlphaVantage) to fetch real-time market data. These third parties have access to your Personal Data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.
  • Legal Obligations: If we are required to respond to legal process, to investigate or remedy potential violations of our policies, or to protect the rights, property, and safety of others, we may share your information as permitted or required by any applicable law, rule, or regulation.

4. Third-Party Technologies

The App relies on the following third-party integrations:

  • Google Firebase: Used for authentication (Firebase Auth), database (Cloud Firestore), and performance monitoring.
  • Apple In-App Purchases: Used to process payments for Corpr Premium. We do not have access to your payment details; Apple securely manages all transactions.
  • AI and Market Data APIs: Used to retrieve financial data and generate automated "Analyst Reviews". Your personal data is not shared with these providers; only the financial ticker symbols are queried.

5. Data Security and Retention

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. Your data is encrypted in transit and stored securely on Google Firebase servers.

  • Private Portfolios: Portfolios marked as "Private" are strictly protected by server-side security rules and cannot be read by other users.
  • Retention: We retain your personal data only for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the App's services.

6. Your Data Protection Rights (GDPR)

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom (UK), you have the following data protection rights:

  • The Right to Access: You can request copies of your personal data.
  • The Right to Rectification: You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You can edit most of your information directly in the App's settings.
  • The Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): You have the right to request that we erase your personal data. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly within the App's Settings menu by tapping "Delete Account".
  • The Right to Restrict Processing: You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data.
  • The Right to Data Portability: You have the right to request that we transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email provided below.

7. Important Financial Disclaimer

Corpr is an informational and educational tool, not a registered financial advisor or broker.

The data, AI-generated "Analyst Reviews", and portfolio tracking tools provided within the App do not constitute financial, investment, legal, or tax advice. You are solely responsible for your own investment decisions. We are not liable for any financial losses or damages incurred from using the App.

8. Policy for Children

The App is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information from our servers immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time in order to reflect changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" of this Privacy Policy. You are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
